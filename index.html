UNCG Campus Food Guide

A quick guide to a few of the best eats within walking distance of UNCG's Petty Building!

Welcome to Your Campus Food Guide!

So your math class or computer science class just ended and you are starving huh? You have a sizable gap before your next class starts so you decide it's probably about time you go and find something to eat. But the question becomes, "Where or what should I eat?" Lucky for you this blog will solve those questions with 3 must try places on campus and within walking distance of the Petty Building!

Each resturant listed here offers some unique variety when it comes down to their menus. Below you will find recomendations, some of my favorite dishes, and maybe even some helpful information to make your decision easy. Lets see what is close by!

Crazy Mexico

Located on Tate Street just steps from campus, Crazy Mexico is a favorite for flavorful Mexican cuisine and generous portions. It offers dine-in and takeout options, making it perfect for quick lunches between classes or relaxed dinners with friends. The hours of operation are 11AM-10PM Monday-Saturday. They are closed on Sundays however.

Choripollo Fajitas

Grilled chicken and chorizo with onions, topped with cheese. Served with beans, rice, guacamoles salad and tortillas.

Crazy Quesadilla

Flour tortilla stuffed with cheese, grilled chicken, steak, shrimp, onions, peppers, guacamole, tomatoes, sour cream and rice.

Texas A.C.P.

Grilled shrimp, steak, chicken, onions, peppers, tomatoes, rice and cheese sauce on top.

Kazoku Japanese Restaurant

Kazoku Sushi is a cozy Japanese restaurant near campus known for fresh sushi and beautifully prepared dishes. It is a great spot for a more relaxed dining experience or a special treat after exams. Hours run from TUES-SAT: 11AM-10PM, SUN: 12PM-9PM, and is closed on Mondays.

Chicken Hibachi

Hibachi Grilled Chicken with: Onion, Carrots, Broccoli, Zucchini, Bell Pepper, White Pepper, Non-dairy Butter. 2oz YumYum(White Sauce) Included, as well as Soup or salad, White rice or Fried Rice

Kanagawa Boat

2 of each Uramaki(Speciality) & Maki(Classic Rolls), 9pcs Sashimi or 10pc sushi(Nigiri). This is served on a giant boat as seen in the image.

Fried Chicken Katsu-Don

Deep-fried Chicken cutlet w/ katsu sauce,pan-fried egg, and cabbage and special katsu sauce. Soup or Salad, White Rice or Fried Rice

Slices by Tony

BBQ Chicken Pizza

Mozzarella cheese, grilled chicken breast, BBQ sauce. You can also add any topping you desire.

Baked Ziti

Pasta mixed with ricotta cheese, baked mozzarella cheese and tomato sauce.

Steak Special Sub

Steak, American cheese, mushrooms, onions, green peppers, lettuce, tomatoes & mayonnaise.

Personal Thoughts and Tips.

  • Out of these restaurants, I find that the one I come to the most ends up being Crazy Mexico as I always end up getting the Texas ACP. It's just so good!
  • By the way, every Tuesdays and Thursdays Crazy Mexico runs a really nice deal where if you bring your student ID and show them you can get 10% off of that meal!
